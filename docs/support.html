Common Questions

How do I play?
Place one star in every row, every column, and every colored region, so that no two stars touch — not even diagonally. There’s always exactly one solution, and you never have to guess.

What does the in-app purchase unlock?
The daily puzzle is always free. The one-time Full Access purchase unlocks unlimited Free Play across all grid sizes. There are no ads and no subscriptions.

I bought Full Access but it’s not showing up.
Open the app’s settings and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re signed in to the same Apple ID you used to buy. If it still doesn’t appear, email us and we’ll sort it out.

Will I lose my garden and streaks?
Your progress is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes that data, so it won’t carry over to a reinstall or a new device.
